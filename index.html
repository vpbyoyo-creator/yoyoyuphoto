About Me 關於我

I walk the streets with a camera and an open mind. Every city has its own rhythm — the way light cuts between buildings at golden hour, the quiet drama of a stranger's silhouette against neon, the beautiful chaos of daily life unfolding in real time.

From intimate portraits to commercial campaigns, I bring the same eye — honest, deliberate, and always looking for the story hiding in plain sight.

我帶著相機走在街頭，帶著一顆開放的心。每座城市都有自己的節奏——黃昏時光線在建築間切割的方式、陌生人的剪影在霓虹前的靜默戲劇、日常生活即時展開的美麗混亂。

從人像到商業攝影，我始終用同一雙眼睛——誠實、有意識地，尋找藏在日常裡的故事。
