About Me 關於我

Photographer based in Taiwan

I started with street photography. Through long hours of observing the city, I gradually developed my way of seeing—recognizing the planes of light, the rhythm of space, and the brief yet precise relationships between people and their surroundings.

That visual language later extended into my portrait and commercial work. Rather than over-stylizing an image, I care more about whether it feels accurate—whether form, content, and emotion can exist within the same visual language. In both personal work and brand collaborations, I want the image to retain a clear structure and a quiet sense of tension.

台灣攝影師

我從街頭開始。在長時間的城市觀察裡，我逐漸建立起自己的拍攝方式：辨認光影的切面、空間的節奏，以及人物與環境之間短暫而準確的關係。

這樣的視覺語言也延伸到後來的人像與商業拍攝。比起過度修飾，我更在意影像是否足夠準確，能否讓形式、內容與情緒站在同一個語言裡。無論是個人創作或品牌合作，我都希望照片保有清楚的結構與安靜的張力。
